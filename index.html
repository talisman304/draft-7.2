Overview

We will study every stage of the smile design process, covering photography, digital smile design, smile try-in, preparation of pink aesthetics, placement, layering, polishing, and the marketing of your aesthetic dentistry.

What you’ll complete

  • Three full maxillary arches of layered composite veneers
  • Workflow from planning and stent fabrication through to finishing and photography
  • Repeatable approach to creating a lifelike, bespoke finish across different smile types

Leading the way

The philosophy behind Iridescent is a counterculture in cosmetic dentistry: refine the natural beauty of teeth, not mask it. This programme is designed to teach layered composite veneers to an exquisite standard—with a focus on smile design, artistic skill, and photography—so you leave confident in planning, placing, finishing and photographing a full arch of composite veneers.

Is this for you?

Designed for

Clinicians who already have a stable restorative skillset and want to refine and elevate composite veneer outcomes.

Not an introduction

This course is advanced and intensive. If you are brand new to composite veneers, the pace may be challenging.

Unsure?

If you are uncertain whether the programme is appropriate, reach out and we can advise based on your experience.

Course breakdown

Each cohort runs from Wednesday to Saturday, starting at 09:00 and finishing at 17:00 daily, with a drinks reception on Saturday.

Day 1 — The theory of smile design

Smile design in real time on your own cases: photography, case planning, smile sketching, Digital Smile Design, 3D printing and palatal stent fabrication, reviewing pink aesthetics, material selection and equipment review, occlusal planning, and placement of palatal shells.

Day 2 — Layering and tooth anatomy

A masterclass in anterior composite placement: tooth preparation, isolation, bonding, shaping, layering, and creation of your first ten-unit case on a normal typodont. We explore the nuances of each tooth and a framework for predictably recreating anatomy in your future cases.

Day 3 — Texturing and polishing your veneers

Refining anatomy, surface texture and polish to the point of perfection—focusing on every detail that makes a tooth appear lifelike. You will finish your first ten-unit case to a world-class standard and begin placement on the next space-closure case.

Day 4 — Creating a masterpiece

Independent polishing of your previous case, photographing and documenting your work, and placement/polishing/photography of your final ten-unit veneer case on a tooth-wear patient. The day closes with a review of all cases, a celebratory drinks reception, and provision of certificates.
